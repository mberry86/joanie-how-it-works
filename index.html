Key principles
What makes it work
01
Notion as source of truth
Every score, scorecard, and analysis lives in Notion. Dashboards read from it — they never replace it. Your data is always yours.
02
Claude does the thinking
Your team brings the data and the question. Claude applies the MPQS formula, scores every publisher, flags the risks, and writes the recommendation.
03
The loop compounds
Every entry in the AI Activity Log makes the next analysis sharper. The system gets better every time your team uses it.
